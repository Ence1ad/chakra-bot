This bot is designed to help you manage and track the time you spend on your activities 🗄 Categories: Categories are used to help organize and group your activities. Use the /help_cat command to learn how to manage categories. 🎬 Actions: Explore the /help_act command to create and manage actions within your categories. ⏱ Trackers: To track your time, go to the "Trackers" menu. Just send /help_tra to learn how to manage trackers. 📊 Reports: Generate reports of your tracked activities. By using the /help_rep, you will learn about managing reports.
